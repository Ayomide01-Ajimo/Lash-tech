Our Story

Founded by Emma Richardson in 2020, Petal & Claw was born from a passion for creating beautiful, unique nail art that empowers clients to express their personal style. With over a decade of experience in the beauty industry, Emma has cultivated a space where creativity and precision meet.

Our philosophy is simple: every client deserves a personalized experience that leaves them feeling confident and beautiful. We use only premium products and stay at the forefront of nail art techniques to ensure your nails are not just decorated, but transformed into wearable art.
